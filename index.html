Have you ever wanted to create your own geocaching game? Well, we think geocaching is too easy - we want to make it a challenge.


Welcome the PuzzleHunt app. Make a a puzzle hunt - a location based scavenger hunt that requires the ability to solve riddles/puzzles to find the next location. Don't feel like making clues? We have some ready for you! We make it easy to make, manage, and play these game - all from the ease of your iPhone.
